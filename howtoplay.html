Snake Game : HOW TO PLAY

Home Play Credit

You can Play Game by follwing below steps

How to Start Game

  1. 1Go to the PLAY page
  2. 2Select gameboard size and game speed
  3. 3Click Start button

Rules

  1. 1There are a gameboard, snake, food
  2. 2The player uses the arrow keys to move the snake on the board.
  3. 3The player gets a point When the snake eats the food.
  4. 4Game over when the snake's head hits its body or wall.
